Comprehensive Overview

DoorBully is a state-of-the-art security solution engineered to protect classrooms and schools during emergency situations. Leveraging advanced locking mechanisms, robust materials, and intelligent sound source localization, DoorBully creates safe havens, ensuring swift and effective lockdowns when every second counts.

With both manual and electronically assisted models, DoorBully seamlessly integrates into existing emergency protocols while enhancing response time and providing unparalleled peace of mind for administrators, teachers, and students.

Electronically Assisted DoorBully

The Electronically Assisted DoorBully integrates cutting-edge technology for remote and centralized control:

  • DC Motor: Powers the slide lock mechanism remotely for instant lockdown.
  • Electronic Control System: Connects to the building’s central unit, enabling real-time status monitoring and machine learning-driven safety analytics.

This model ensures immediate responses to threats, offering a powerful tool for administrators to secure multiple rooms simultaneously.

Manual DoorBully

The Manual DoorBully provides a mechanically operated, reliable solution:

  • Rail System: Adapts to all standard door frames, ensuring proper positioning.
  • Slide Lock: Manually secure the door with a simple, robust mechanism.
  • Status Indicators: Visual cues offer immediate confirmation of lock status.
  • High-Strength Materials: Carbon fiber and PLA provide strength, durability, and lightweight handling.

It’s a straightforward, cost-effective option for institutions looking to enhance security without electronic infrastructure.

Sound Source Localization

DoorBully features advanced sound source localization, leveraging strategically placed microphones and AI-driven algorithms:

  • Precision Placement: Microphones placed according to digital floor plans for accurate triangulation.
  • Acoustic Analysis: Detects specific decibel thresholds and frequencies (e.g., gunshots), minimizing false positives.
  • Real-Time Data Integration: Triangulates sound origin to pinpoint threats instantly.
  • AI Model Training: Simulations refine detection capabilities, using Rainforest Regression for complex audio pattern analysis.

Installation Process

DoorBully’s installation is designed for simplicity and speed, ensuring minimal disruption:

  • Custom Mounting Solutions: Brackets align seamlessly with door frames.
  • Server and Network Setup: A secure, UPS-backed server manages and monitors all DoorBully units, connected through reliable mesh networks.
  • User-Friendly Interface: A GUI enables quick configuration of classroom setups, device parameters, and security protocols.

Testing & Results

Rigorous endurance testing ensures long-term reliability:

  • Cycle Tests: Simulated 22,500 operations to assess mechanical and electronic durability.
  • Material Performance: Evaluated Carbon Fiber Nylon Composites, PLA, and Billet Aluminum 6061. Aluminum displayed minimal wear and superior longevity.

Future Production Plans

DoorBully’s roadmap includes scaling production and enhancing material and component quality:

  • Gravity Die Casting: For durable, corrosion-resistant metal parts at high volume.
  • Injection Molding: Ensures precise, high-quality components using gas-assisted techniques.
  • Modular PCBs: Simplifies component replacement and future upgrades.
  • ADA-Compliant Design: Handles and interfaces designed for accessibility and compliance with Americans with Disabilities Act standards.

Market Insights

DoorBully addresses a critical and growing need in a volatile security landscape:

  • Increasing Need: School shootings have risen dramatically, underscoring the urgency for robust solutions.
  • Fire Code Compliance: DoorBully meets safety regulations, unlike many supplementary locks.
  • Global Security Market: Projected to reach $483.62 billion by 2026, signaling strong demand for advanced solutions.

Field Implementation

DoorBully is moving from concept to community:

  • Pilot Programs: 10 units installed in local schools to gather real-world feedback.
  • District Collaboration: Partnerships with 11 school districts for planned system rollouts.
  • Large-Scale Deployments: 45 units approved for installation, pending final testing and evaluation.

Conclusion

DoorBully revolutionizes classroom security with its robust mechanical and electronic solutions, advanced sound localization capabilities, and streamlined installation and management processes. By evolving continuously through feedback and testing, DoorBully stands ready to set new standards in educational safety, ensuring that students and staff are protected in the moments that matter most.
